2) Industry Carousels

General behavior for all three rows:
	•	Each row is a horizontal infinite carousel of cards.
	•	No arrows, no dots. Slow, continuous scroll for readability.
	•	Row 1 scrolls left → right.
	•	Row 2 scrolls right → left (opposite direction).
	•	Row 3 scrolls left → right again.
	•	On mobile, each row should show one card at a time and still loop slowly.
	•	Cards are tappable/hoverable but do not need deep links yet.

Each card has the same internal structure:
	•	Small industry label chip.
	•	Title (industry name).
	•	Short 1–2 sentence blurb.
	•	Two compact bullet lists:
	•	“Where operations drag”
	•	“Where AI actually fits”

Use the copy below as the content for each card.
